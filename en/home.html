We warmly welcome you to our “Wolke7” in Bad Harzburg. We wish you a restful and relaxing holiday in the Harz Mountains.

Our charming holiday apartment “Wolke7” welcomes you in Bad Harzburg, 1,7 kilometres from the center of the spa town of Bad Harzburg. It's a modern furnished 2 bedroom apartment with the character of a detached house where you can relax, play golf and go hiking.

Enjoy the 4 star apartment built in a bungalow style with a spacious South-west terrace, garden furniture and a table grill. Our apartment “Wolke7” was completely renovated in 2022 and offers our guests a very high standard. A high-quality kitchen consists a dishwasher, coffee machine, Nespresso machine, oven, toaster and a mixer. The apartment has a bedroom with a double bed and a living room with a high-quality sofa bed for two guests. The beautiful bathroom consists a shower, hairdryer and a large mirror. The terrace is accessible via the living room and the bedroom. Unfortunately dogs aren't allowed.
